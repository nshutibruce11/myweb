Who We Are

Citizen Diplomacy for Regional Stability

The Great Lakes Peace Initiative Africa (GLOPIA) is a citizen diplomacy initiative adding its voice to critical frameworks like the UN Great Lakes Strategic Framework (UN GLRSF). We operate in Burundi, DRC, Rwanda, and Uganda—regions historically affected by prolonged conflict.

We collaborate with initiatives like the Great Lakes Peace Initiative Centre (GLPIC) and the African Great Lakes Initiative (AGLI) to foster human rights, democracy, and knowledge.

Our Mission: To foster peace, stability, and development in the Africa Great Lakes region in accordance with the Africa Agenda 2063 "The Africa We Want".

Key Focus Areas

Our strategic pillars for sustainable impact.

Youth & Women Empowerment

Developing leadership and capacities for peacebuilding through training, mentorship, and engagement with decision-makers.

Socio-Economic Development

Linking peacebuilding with economic development to create sustainable stability and resilience in conflict-affected areas.

Cross-Border Cooperation

Promoting dialogue between communities and authorities across borders to reinforce regional peacebuilding efforts.

Justice & Reconciliation

Addressing accountability for past violence and promoting reconciliation processes to heal communities.

Local Peace Building

Acknowledging community-based initiatives addressing root causes like land and identity conflicts.

Human Rights & Democracy

Building a foundation for lasting peace through the promotion and protection of fundamental human rights.

Our Projects

Transforming lives through sustainable enterprise.

Social Enterprise

Coffee for Peace Great Lakes (CPGL)

A social enterprise founded in October 2025 by GLOPIA Ltd. It uses coffee to build peace within the Great Lakes region, promoting the Great Lakes Coffee Blend (GLCB).

How it works

  • Trains communities to produce and sell high-quality coffee.
  • Empowers farmers to become ‘Agripreneurs’.
  • Provides specialty coffee as an alternative to the illicit economy.
  • Focuses on peacebuilding, conflict transformation, and business management.

Impact

Creates stable livelihoods, fosters reconciliation between communities, and supports families across the value chain from Burundi, DRC, Rwanda, and Uganda.
